Local Search Algrithm（局部搜索算法）#

Local Search(局部搜索)的过程#

  • 算法从一个或者若干个初始解出发
  • 在算法中参数的控制下由当前状态的邻域中产生若干个候选解
  • 以某种策略在候选解中确定新的当前解
  • 重复执行上述搜索过程，直到满足算法终止条件；在这期间伴随参数的调节
  • 结束搜索过程，并输出最终结果

Local Search(局部搜索)的几大要素#

  • 目标函数：用来判断解的优劣
  • 邻域的定义：根据不同的问题，采用不同的邻域定义方式
  • 初始解的产生方法
  • 迭代过程中新解的产生和接受规则
  • 迭代终止条件

Simulated Annealing（模拟退火算法）#

伪代码如下：

\begin{aligned} & s \leftarrow GenerateInitialSolution() \\ & T \leftarrow T_0 \\ & while \enspace termination \enspace conditions \enspace not \enspace met \enspace do \\ & \qquad s' \leftarrow PickAtRandom(N(s)) \\ & \qquad if \enspace (f(s') < f(s)) \enspace then \\ & \qquad \qquad s \leftarrow s' \qquad \qquad \# \enspace use \enspace s' \enspace replaces \enspace s \\ & \qquad else \\ & \qquad \qquad Accept \enspace s' \enspace as \enspace new \enspace solution \enspace with \enspace probability \enspace p(T, s', s) \\ & \qquad endif \\ & \qquad Update(T) \\ & endwhile \end{aligned}

TABU/TABOO Search（TS、禁忌搜索）#

伪代码如下：

\begin{aligned} & s \leftarrow GenerateInitialSolution() \\ & InitializeTabuLists(TL_1, TL_2, ... , TL_r) \\ & k \leftarrow 0 \\ & while \enspace termination \enspace condition \enspace not \enspace met \enspace do \\ & \qquad AllowedSet(s, k) \leftarrow \{s' \in N(s) | s \enspace does \enspace not \enspace violate \enspace a \enspace tabu \enspace condition, \\ & \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad \qquad or \enspace it \enspace satisfies \enspace at \enspace least \enspace one \enspace aspiration \enspace condition \} \\ & \qquad s \leftarrow ChooseBestOf(AllowedSet(s, k)) \\ & \qquad UpdateTabuListsAndAspirationConditions() \\ & \qquad k \leftarrow k + 1 \\ & endwhile \end{aligned}

Hill_Climbing algorithm for minimization problem

Procedure HillClimbing():
    x := getInitial(); // get initial feasible solution x
    while (the stopping condition is not reached):
        x' = localSearch(Neighborhood(x)); // select the optimal solution from the neighborhood of x as x'
        if (c'x' <= c'x):
            x := x'
        else:
            return x
    end
