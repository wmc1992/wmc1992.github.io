ARC easy and challenge#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1803.05457.pdf

Clark et al., 2018

该数据集主要是小学科学问题，共计7787个问题。划分为了简单集（ARC Easy）5197个问题，挑战集（ARC Challenge）2590个问题。下面是该数据集的一些样例。

Which element makes up most of the air we breathe? (A) carbon (B) nitrogen (C) oxygen (D) argon 我们呼吸的空气主要由哪种元素构成？ (A) 碳 (B) 氮气 (C) 氧气 (D) 氩气
The crust, the mantle, and the core are structures of Earth. Which description is a feature of Earth’s mantle? (A) contains fossil remains (B) consists of tectonic plates (C) is located at the center of Earth (D) has properties of both liquids and solids 地壳、地幔和地核是地球的结构。 下列哪一项描述是地幔的特征？ (A) 包含化石遗迹 (B) 由构造板块组成 (C) 位于地球中心 (D) 具有液体和固体特性
What is the first step of the process in the formation of sedimentary rocks? (A) erosion (B) deposition (C) compaction (D) cementation 沉积岩形成过程的第一步是什么？ (A) 侵蚀 (B) 沉积 (C) 压实 (D) 胶结

CommonsenseQA#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1811.00937.pdf

Talmor et al., 2018

总共 12247 个常识问答数据。该数据集是选择题的形式，每个问题有五个选项。该数据集的特点是：需要知道一些概念的固有属性才能够回答。比如说关于"河流"这个概念，模型可能需要知道"河流里面有水"这个信息才能够回答问题，这个信息并不出现在问题或答案中，需要模型自己知道。

下面根据下表步骤a和步骤b的截图来说明该数据集的构造方式：

  • 如步骤a的图片所示，在 ConceptNet 中采样一个源概念和三个目标概念，在步骤a的图中，river 是源概念，waterfall、bridge、valley是目标概念。

  • 如步骤b的图片所示，众包人员根据一个源概念和一个目标概念编写一条数据，步骤b的图片中就是根据三组概念(river, waterfall)、(river, bridge)、(river, valley)编写的三条数据。

  • 众包人员再给每条编写的数据从 ConceptNet 中采样一个概念作为混淆的概念，即步骤b图片中红色单词。

  • 最后众包人员再给每条编写的数据自己写一个混淆概念，即步骤b图片中紫色单词。

BoolQ#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1905.10044.pdf

Clark et al., 2019

数据集BoolQ中的每条数据由一个question (Q)、一个passage (P)、一个answer (A)组成。其涵盖的主题有娱乐媒体、自然科学、体育、法律政府、历史、小说等。

BoolQ样本举例如下

Q: Has the UK been hit by a hurricane?

P: The Great Storm of 1987 was a violent extratropical cyclone which caused casualties in England, France and the Channel Islands ...

A: Yes. [An example event is given.]

Q: 英国遭受飓风袭击了吗？

P: 1987年的大风暴是一场猛烈的温带气旋，在英国、法国和海峡群岛造成人员伤亡...

A: Yes. [给出了一个示例事件。]

Q: Does France have a Prime Minister and a President?

P: ... The extent to which those decisions lie with the Prime Minister or President depends upon ...

A: Yes. [Both are mentioned, so it can be inferred both exist.]

Q: 法国有总理和总统吗？

P: ...这些决定在多大程度上由总理或总统取决于...

A: Yes. [两者都被提及，因此可以推断两者都存在。]

Q: Have the San Jose Sharks won a Stanley Cup?

P: ... The Sharks have advanced to the Stanley Cup finals once, losing to the Pittsburgh Penguins in 2016 ...

A: No. [They were in the finals once, and lost.]

Q: 圣何塞鲨鱼队赢得过斯坦利杯吗？

P: ...鲨鱼队曾一度晋级斯坦利杯决赛，但在2016年输给了匹兹堡企鹅队...

A: No. [他们曾经进入过决赛，但输了。]

HellaSwag#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1905.07830.pdf

Zellers et al., 2019

该数据集是采用 Adversarial Filtering (AF) 筛选出来的模型预测效果较差的一个常识推理数据集，按照论文所说人工判断时能够得到95%的指标。不过就个人感觉这个数据集还挺难的，下面是这个数据集的两个数据样例。比如第一个样例，问题文本为："一个女人带着一个水桶和一只狗在外面。 狗到处乱跑，试图避免洗澡。"，接下来要从四个选项中选取这个人将会如何做，正确的答案是她试图给狗洗澡，把狗弄湿，然后狗不想洗澡，又跑了。

和之前的常识推理不同的是，之前较简单的常识推理的答案是唯一且固定的，该数据集的答案则变成了最合理的那一个选项。

Question: A woman is outside with a bucket and a dog. The dog is running around trying to avoid a bath. She...

Answers:

  • A. rinses the bucket off with soap and blow dry the dog’s head.
  • B. uses a hose to keep it from getting soapy.
  • C. gets the dog wet, then it runs away again. - ✔
  • D. gets into a bath tub with the dog.

Question: 一个女人带着一个水桶和一只狗在外面。狗到处乱跑，试图避免洗澡。她...

Answers:

  • A. 用肥皂冲洗水桶并吹干狗的头。
  • B. 使用软管防止其沾上肥皂。
  • C. 把狗弄湿了，然后它又跑了。 - ✔
  • D. 和狗一起进入浴缸。

Question: Come to a complete halt at a stop sign or red light. At a stop sign, come to a complete halt for about 2 seconds or until vehicles that arrived before you clear the intersection. If you're stopped at a red light, proceed when the light has turned green. ...

Answers:

  • A. Stop for no more than two seconds, or until the light turns yellow. A red light in front of you indicates that you should stop.
  • B. After you come to a complete stop, turn off your turn signal. Allow vehicles to move in different directions before moving onto the sidewalk.
  • C. Stay out of the oncoming traffic. People coming in from behind may elect to stay left or right.
  • D. If the intersection has a white stripe in your lane, stop before this line. Wait until all traffic has cleared before crossing the intersection. - ✔

Question: 在停车标志或红灯处完全停车。 在停车标志处，完全停车约 2 秒钟，或者直到车辆到达后您才通过路口。 如果您在红灯处停下来，请在灯变绿时继续行驶。 ...

Answers:

  • A. 停止时间不超过两秒，或直到指示灯变黄。 您面前的红灯表示您应该停车。
  • B. 完全停下来后，关闭转向灯。 在驶入人行道之前，允许车辆向不同方向行驶。
  • C. 远离迎面驶来的车辆。 从后面进来的人可能会选择留在左边或右边。
  • D. 如果十字路口的车道上有白色条纹，请在此线之前停车。 等到所有交通畅通后再穿过十字路口。 - ✔

SIQA#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1904.09728.pdf

Sap et al., 2019

针对社交场景下的常识推理数据集，总共38000个多项选择题。下面是该数据集中关于不同推理内容的三个例子。

reasoning about motivation 关于动机的推理

Context: Tracy had accidentally pressed upon Austin in the small elevator and it was awkward.

Question: Why did Tracy do this?

Answers:

  • (a) get very close to Austin
  • (b) squeeze into the elevator - ✔
  • (c) get flirty with Austin

Context: 特蕾西在小电梯里不小心压到了奥斯汀，场面很尴尬。

Question: 特蕾西为什么要这么做？

Answers:

  • (a) 非常接近奥斯汀
  • (b) 挤进电梯 - ✔
  • (c) 与奥斯汀调情
reasoning about what happens next 推理接下来会发生什么

Context: Alex spilled the food she just prepared all over the floor and it made a huge mess.

Question: What will Alex want to do next?

Answers:

  • (a) taste the food
  • (b) mop up - ✔
  • (c) run around in the mess

Context: 亚历克斯把她刚准备的食物洒了一地，弄得一团糟。

Question: 亚历克斯下一步想做什么？

Answers:

  • (a) 品尝食物
  • (b) 拖地 - ✔
  • (c) 在混乱中跑来跑去
reasoning about emotional reactions 对情绪反应的推理

Context: In the school play, Robin played a hero in the struggle to the death with the angry villain.

Question: How would others feel afterwards?

Answers:

  • (a) sorry for the villain
  • (b) hopeful that Robin will succeed - ✔
  • (c) like Robin should lose

Context: 在学校的戏剧中，罗宾扮演了一位与愤怒的恶棍进行殊死搏斗的英雄。

Question: 事后其他人会有什么感受？

Answers:

  • (a) 对恶棍感到抱歉
  • (b) 希望罗宾能够成功 - ✔
  • (c) 希望罗宾输

Commonsense Convenience

[Goal] How to make sure all the clocks in the house are set accurately?

[Sol1] Get a solar clock for a reference and place it just outside a window that gets lots of sun. Use a system of call and response once a month, having one person stationed at the solar clock who yells out the correct time and have another person move to each of the indoor clocks to check if they are showing the right time. Adjust as necessary. - ✗

[Sol2] Replace all wind-ups with digital clocks. That way, you set them once, and that’s it. Check the batteries once a year or if you notice anything looks a little off. - ✔

[Goal] 如何确保家里所有的时钟都设置准确？

[Sol1] 准备一个太阳能时钟作为参考，并将其放置在阳光充足的窗外。 每月使用一次呼叫和响应系统，让一个人站在太阳钟前喊出正确的时间，并让另一个人走到每个室内时钟前检查它们是否显示正确的时间。 根据需要进行调整。 - ✗

[Sol2] 将所有发条更换为数字时钟。 这样，您只需设置一次即可。 每年检查一次电池，或者如果您发现任何东西看起来有点不对劲。 - ✔

WinoGrande#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1907.10641.pdf

三、阅读理解#

阅读理解（Reading Comprehension）：

RACE#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/1704.04683.pdf

Lai et al., 2017

一个阅读理解任务数据集，数据集来自中国中学生英语考试，涵盖多种主题，用于评估理解与推理能力。数据格式如下所示，每条数据中有一段（Passage）英文文本，几个选择题（Questions），和这些选择题的答案（Answer）。

RACE数据集样本举例

Passage:

In a small village in England about 150 years ago, a mail coach was standing on the street. It didn’t come to that village often. People had to pay a lot to get a letter. The person who sent the letter didn’t have to pay the postage, while the receiver had to. “Here’s a letter for Miss Alice Brown,” said the mailman.

“ I’m Alice Brown,” a girl of about 18 said in a low voice.

Alice looked at the envelope for a minute, and then handed it back to the mailman.

“I’m sorry I can’t take it, I don’t have enough money to pay it”, she said.

A gentleman standing around were very sorry for her. Then he came up and paid the postage for her.

When the gentleman gave the letter to her, she said with a smile, “ Thank you very much, This letter is from Tom. I’m going to marry him. He went to London to look for work. I’ve waited a long time for this letter, but now I don’t need it, there is nothing in it.”

“Really? How do you know that?” the gentleman said in surprise.

“He told me that he would put some signs on the envelope. Look, sir, this cross in the corner means that he is well and this circle means he has found work. That’s good news.”

The gentleman was Sir Rowland Hill. He didn’t forgot Alice and her letter.

“The postage to be paid by the receiver has to be changed,” he said to himself and had a good plan.

“The postage has to be much lower, what about a penny? And the person who sends the letter pays the postage. He has to buy a stamp and put it on the envelope.” he said . The government accepted his plan. Then the first stamp was put out in 1840. It was called the “Penny Black”. It had a picture of the Queen on it.

Questions:

1): The first postage stamp was made _ .

A. in England B. in America C. by Alice D. in 1910

2): The girl handed the letter back to the mailman because _ .

A. she didn’t know whose letter it was

B. she had no money to pay the postage

C. she received the letter but she didn’t want to open it

D. she had already known what was written in the letter

3): We can know from Alice’s words that _ .

A. Tom had told her what the signs meant before leaving

B. Alice was clever and could guess the meaning of the signs

C. Alice had put the signs on the envelope herself

D. Tom had put the signs as Alice had told him to

4): The idea of using stamps was thought of by _ .

A. the government

B. Sir Rowland Hill

C. Alice Brown

D. Tom

5): From the passage we know the high postage made _ .

A. people never send each other letters

B. lovers almost lose every touch with each other

C. people try their best to avoid paying it

D. receivers refuse to pay the coming letters

Answer: ADABC

七、C-EVAL#

论文链接: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2305.08322.pdf

Huang et al., 2023

该数据集是一个中文的多层次多学科评估数据集。多层次指该数据集中问题包含：初中（middle school）、高中（high school）、大学（college）、以及更具专业性（professional）的问题。多学科指包含四个大类：STEM、社会科学（social science）、人文（humanity）、其他（other），对应52个具体的学科。

该数据集总共包含13948道选择题。C-EVAL HARD 是该数据集的子集，主要是该数据集中更困难的数据。

该数据集的数据样例如下所示：

洛伦兹曲线越是向横轴凸出____
A. 基尼系数就越大，收入就越不平等
B. 基尼系数就越大，收入就越平等
C. 基尼系数就越小，收入就越不平等
D. 基尼系数就越小，收入就越平等

答案:A

同时为了能够应用COT的能力，该数据集中也会生成答案解析，该解析并不是完全由人工编写，而是先通过目前的SOTA模型（比如GPT-4）生成解析，再加上人工校验得到，例子如下：

25°C时，将pH=2的强酸溶液与pH=13的强碱溶液混合，所得混合液的pH=11，则强酸溶液与强碱溶液 的体积比是(忽略混合后溶液的体积变化)____
A. 11:1 B. 9:1 C. 1:11 D. 1:9

答案:B

答案解析：
1. PH=13的强碱性溶液中，PH=2的强酸性溶液中，酸碱混合后PH=11，即。
2. 设强酸和强碱溶液的体积分别为x和y，则，解得。
